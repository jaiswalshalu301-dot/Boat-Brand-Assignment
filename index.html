THE EXECUTION

You are not building a “community”. You are building a digital system that gets young users to the site, engages them beyond purchase, and makes them talk about the brand.

01. MEMBERSHIP & EXCLUSIVE DROPS

Create a members-only layer on top of boAt that gives users a sense of "I get something others don't". Users enter via website signup, purchase, or contest participation.

  • Early Access: Priority on launches and deals.
  • Special Discounts: Not visible to the public.
  • Exclusive Watch Drops: Launch limited collections only available on the website in limited quantity. Positioned as "not for everyone."

Why it matters: This gives people a reason to come to the website directly, shifting the product to desirable, not just functional.

02. INSTAGRAM STRATEGY OVERHAUL

The current problem in the category: Too many product shots. Too many features. We need to shift communication from "Here’s our watch" to "This is how it looks on you."

What you should show:

  • People wearing watches with curated outfits.
  • Close wrist shots with intricate clothing styling.
  • Real-life usage mapping (gym, college, office, high-end outings).

Separate The Handles:

Main page = Brand sales & comms. New page = Style + Culture (Styling content, drop announcements, creator collabs).

03. ONLINE CONTESTS & INFLUENCERS

Contests are not random — they must be purpose-driven. Our Influencer play drops massive celebrities because they don't influence buying in this specific wearable category.

Example: "Style Your Watch"

Users post their outfit + watch and tag the brand. They get a chance to be featured in the FASH-TECH campaign. This creates aspiration and massive UGC.

Influencer Playbook:

  • Style Creators: Outfit-focused content. (e.g., @rebleofficial - baddies edge)
  • Cool Aesthetics: Siddhant Chaturvedi-esque vibes.
  • Fitness Creators: Gym, lifestyle, and athleisure.

WEBSITE CHANGES

Right now: Looks like a product listing site. Needs to change to: Look like a fashion-tech brand.

01

Homepage Re-design

Show people, not just products. Focus entirely on styling over functional spec-sheets.

02

Product Pages

Add "Best for your style" module. Inject outfit pairing visuals and show the watch in real-life use settings.

03

Watch Faces

Don't show faces as technical features. Categorize them as mood-based choices: Minimal, Loud, Gym, Night.

04

Fixing The Journey

Drives traffic directly to your website rather than marketplaces and sets up identity-based positioning.
